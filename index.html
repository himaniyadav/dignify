About Us

Hi! We're Dignify. We're working to alleviate homelessness in a revolutionary way. We're empowering those experiencing homelessness by giving them the tools to find the resources they need to get by. Whether it's transportation, meals, healthcare, or a bed for the night, Dignify can point in the right direction. Our smart analytics system allows for updating information in real time - reserving a spot at a shelter or accessing finding free flu shots are all as easy as the click of a button. And Dignify does this all without collecting any personally identifying data. We pride ourselves on connecting service providers and governments directly to the homeless network, creating smarter solutions.
